# SARS Requirements

## Overview

Documents how the system enforces South African Revenue Service tax compliance requirements for expense deductions.

## Key Provisions

- **S11(a) Substantiation**: Mandatory receipt uploads, business purpose descriptions
- **VAT Invoice Thresholds**: R50 (simplified) / R5,000 (full tax invoice) -- affects receipt validation
- **Tax Administration Act S29**: 5-year record retention requirement
- **S8(1)(a)**: Travel allowance provisions for long-distance travel
