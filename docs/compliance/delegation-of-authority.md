# Delegation of Authority

## Overview

Documents the tiered delegation of authority structure, aligned to King IV Principle 7, and summarises the Two-Key Threshold Authorization System specification.

## Tier Structure

| Tier | Role | Monetary Limit | Authority |
|------|------|---------------|-----------|
| 1 | Line Manager | Up to R999.99 | Final approval for routine expenses |
| 2 | Head of Department | Up to R10,000.00 | Final approval for escalated expenses |

## Two-Key Threshold Authorization (Summary)

The Two-Key system ensures no single approver can unilaterally approve claims above their authority. See `enhancements/two-key-threshold-auth.md` for the full specification.
